2-2. Information Commonly Collected (via Ad SDK)

For our Apps that display advertisements, the Google AdMob SDK collects and transmits the following information:

  • Advertising ID (Android Advertising ID / AAID)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, OS version, language, time zone)
  • Approximate location (country level, based on IP address)
  • App interaction and ad viewing logs

3. Purpose of Use

Pursuant to Article 17 of the APPI, we use the collected information only within the scope of the following purposes:

  • To provide the functionality of our Apps (recording, displaying, and aggregating data entered by users)
  • To deliver and optimize advertisements (via Google AdMob)
  • To analyze and improve app stability and quality (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • To respond to inquiries
  • To notify users of changes to this Policy and other communications

If we use the collected information for purposes other than those above, we will obtain the user's prior consent.

4. Third-Party Disclosure (List of Recipient SDKs)

Our Apps use the following third-party services (SDKs), and user data is transmitted to each service. In no case do we directly transmit a user's name, address, telephone number, or email address.

4-1. Google AdMob

  • Provider: Google LLC
  • Recipient Country: United States of America
  • Information Transmitted: Advertising ID (AAID), IP address, device information (model, OS version), approximate location (country level), app interaction and ad viewing logs
  • Purpose of Transmission: Ad delivery, fraud/invalid click detection, performance measurement
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Advertising Policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4-2. Firebase Crashlytics

  • Provider: Google LLC
  • Recipient Country: United States of America
  • Information Transmitted: Stack traces upon crash, device information, app identifier, Firebase Installation ID, app state
  • Purpose of Transmission: Analysis and improvement of app stability and quality
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

If we introduce additional SDKs required for app-specific features (such as voice recognition, cloud sync, or in-app billing), we will amend this Policy and apply them only to the relevant apps.

5. Advertising

Some of our Apps display advertisements via Google AdMob. The handling of data associated with ad delivery is as described in Section 4-1.

5-1. Consent in the EU/EEA and UK (UMP)

For users in the EU/EEA and the UK, we obtain consent regarding the use of data for advertising purposes at first launch via the Google User Messaging Platform (UMP). Users may choose between "personalized advertising" or "non-personalized advertising", and may change their preference at any time via the "Reconfigure consent settings" menu within the app.

5-2. Resetting the Advertising ID

Users can reset or delete the Advertising ID at any time via "Settings → Privacy → Ads" on their Android device.

6. Handling of Sensitive Personal Information

None of our currently published apps handle information that may fall under "sensitive personal information" (要配慮個人情報) as defined in Article 2, paragraph 3 of the APPI (e.g., medical history, physical or mental disability, results of medical examinations by a physician, etc.).

If we publish an app that handles such information in the future, we will keep that information strictly within the user's device and will not transmit it to us or any third party. The amended details will be reflected in this Policy.

7. Children's Privacy

In line with the spirit of Google Play's Families Policy and the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), we strive to protect the personal information of children. We do not currently publish any app primarily targeting children under the age of 13.

If we become aware that a child under the age of 13 has inadvertently provided personal information, we will promptly delete it. Please contact contact@akiappsdev.com.

8. Data Storage and Retention Period

  • On-device data: Completely deleted when the user uninstalls the app. We do not have any means to back up or recover such data.
  • Data via Google AdMob / Firebase Crashlytics: Retained in accordance with Google LLC's data retention policies. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for details.
  • Inquiry records: We delete received emails approximately one year after the inquiry has been resolved.

9. Disclosure, Correction, and Deletion Requests (APPI Articles 28–31)

Pursuant to Articles 28 through 31 of the APPI, users may make the following requests regarding the personal data we retain:

  • Notification of purpose of use
  • Disclosure of retained personal data (Article 28)
  • Correction, addition, or deletion of retained personal data (Article 29)
  • Cessation of use, erasure, or cessation of third-party provision of retained personal data (Article 30)
  • Disclosure of records of third-party provision (Article 28, paragraph 5)

How to Submit a Request: Please contact contact@akiappsdev.com with the following information:

  • Type of request (disclosure / correction / deletion, etc.)
  • Name of the relevant app
  • Details of the request
  • Information for identity verification (email address, Google account, or other identifying information)

Estimated Response Time: We will respond in writing or by email within two weeks (or within 30 days for complex matters) after receiving the request. Please note that the majority of our Apps store data only on the device, and the personal data we retain and manage is therefore limited.

Fees: Responding to disclosure requests is free of charge. However, if you request a written response by post, you may be asked to bear the actual postage costs.

11. Security Measures

To prevent the leakage, loss, or damage of user data we collect, we implement the following security measures:

  • Technical safeguards: Data entered by users is, in principle, stored on the device and is not transmitted externally. Where external communication is necessary, only encrypted communication via HTTPS (TLS) is used. Cloud services such as Firebase rely on Google's encrypted storage and access controls.
  • Organizational safeguards: We operate as a sole proprietorship, and user data obtained in the course of business is handled solely by the proprietor. No business operations are outsourced to third parties.
  • Physical safeguards: Development devices are protected by password / biometric lock and full-disk encryption.
  • Personnel safeguards: The proprietor continuously engages in self-directed study of the APPI and the GDPR.
